- Our Motto -

- Here at Chronos Ranch, we work hard to deliver you the best, top-notch quality of beef, dairy, lamb, and poultry. Our employees have pledged to never deliver a spoiled meal or damaged product, as they are very diligent in their craft. We here at Chronos Ranch have been shipping non-organic, GMO livestock for decades, starting back in Amarillo Texas, 1998. Then, starting in the 2000s, we introduced new livestock in the form of sheep. After many years of dedication and perseverance we finally became the global cattle company we've been known as today. All that time, the motto, our motto we have worked by for over 30 years, is quick, cheap, and reliable. We maintain a relatively low price by the usage of GMO organisms, using them to provide heaps of meat and dairy. Doing this we have a fast stream of fast, keeping prices a mininum, and always having a plenty amount of food to supply to our dear customers. That is our way at Chronos Ranch.

Our Name

Our name, 'Chronos Ranch', comes from kroʊnɒs, the Greek god of time, or the Greek word for time and ranch, for the fact the company originated from a humble ranch. Focusing on the Chronos part, we(I) chose it because Chronos, or time represents the refrigeration process we use to freeze our meat and send across the country into your local supermarket to store for 8 - 24 months. (And also how it's my 4th favorite word)
